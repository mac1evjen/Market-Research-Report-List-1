<p><p>The 3-axis CNC machining centers are categorized into vertical and horizontal types, each serving distinct manufacturing needs. Vertical 3-axis machines have a vertically oriented spindle, ideal for precision drilling and milling tasks on smaller workpieces. Conversely, horizontal 3-axis machines feature a horizontally oriented spindle, which excels in cutting larger parts and achieving better chip removal during the machining process. Both types enhance efficiency and accuracy in various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and general manufacturing.</p></p>
