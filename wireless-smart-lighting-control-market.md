<p><ul><li>Zigbee</li><li>Wifi</li><li>Bluetooth</li><li>Z-Wave</li><li>Enocean</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireless smart lighting control market comprises various technologies, including Zigbee, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, Z-Wave, and EnOcean. Zigbee is known for low power consumption and mesh networking, making it ideal for large installations. Wi-Fi offers robust connectivity and is widely used but can consume more energy. Bluetooth is favored for its simplicity and short-range applications. Z-Wave is designed for home automation with reliable long-range communication. EnOcean specializes in energy-harvesting technology, allowing devices to operate without batteries, promoting sustainability.</p></p>
