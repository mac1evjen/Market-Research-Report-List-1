<p><p>The automotive valves market comprises various types essential for vehicle performance. Solenoid valves control fluid flow electronically, enhancing response times. EGR (Exhaust Gas Recirculation) valves reduce emissions by recirculating exhaust gases back into the engine. Brake combination valves ensure optimal brake fluid distribution and safety. AT (Automatic Transmission) control valves manage fluid flow in automatic transmissions for smooth gear shifts. Engine valves, including intake and exhaust valves, regulate air and fuel intake and gas expulsion, crucial for engine efficiency.</p></p>
