<p><strong>The Wearable AMOLED Driver ICs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>COG Package IC</li><li>COF Package IC</li><li>COP Package IC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wearable AMOLED driver IC market is segmented into three main package types: COG (Chip on Glass), COF (Chip on Film), and COP (Chip on Panel). COG integrates the driver directly onto the glass substrate, enhancing display slimness, while COF mounts the IC on a flexible film, allowing for lightweight and adaptable designs. COP positions the driver on the display panel itself, offering improved electrical performance and thermal management. Each type caters to specific integration needs, flexibility, and design requirements in wearable technology.</p></p>
