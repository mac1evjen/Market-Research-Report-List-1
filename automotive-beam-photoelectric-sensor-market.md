<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automotive Beam (Photoelectric) Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Through-Beam Photoelectric Sensor</li><li>Reflective Photoelectric Sensor</li><li>Diffuse Photoelectric Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive beam (photoelectric) sensor market includes three main types: </p><p>1. **Through-Beam Photoelectric Sensors** transmit a beam of light between an emitter and a receiver, detecting obstacles based on interruption of the beam. </p><p>2. **Reflective Photoelectric Sensors** send light toward a reflective surface, sensing changes when an object blocks the light from returning. </p><p>3. **Diffuse Photoelectric Sensors** emit light and detect it after reflecting directly off nearby objects, suitable for short-range applications. Each type serves distinct automotive safety and automation functions.</p></p>
