<p><strong>The Battery Laminated Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Process</li><li>Dry Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Battery Laminated Film market is primarily categorized into two types: Thermal Process and Dry Process. The Thermal Process involves the application of heat to bond layers of film, ensuring strong adhesion and moisture resistance, making it suitable for high-performance batteries. In contrast, the Dry Process utilizes solvent-free adhesives to achieve bonding at room temperature, offering benefits like faster production times and reduced environmental impact. Both processes are essential for enhancing battery efficiency and longevity in various applications.</p></p>
