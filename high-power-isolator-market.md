<p><p>The High Power Isolator market is segmented into Single-Stage and Dual-Stage types based on their design and performance. Single-Stage isolators utilize a single non-reciprocal device to prevent signal reflection, making them compact and cost-effective, ideal for lower-power applications. In contrast, Dual-Stage isolators employ two non-reciprocal devices to enhance isolation and functionality, offering superior performance in high-power scenarios, making them suitable for demanding applications like telecommunications and industrial systems where signal integrity is critical.</p></p>
