<p><p>Functional sugar for dietary supplements encompasses various types, serving distinct health benefits. Oligosaccharides, such as fructooligosaccharides, promote gut health by acting as prebiotics. Inulin, a soluble fiber, aids digestion and assists in weight management. Sugar alcohols like xylitol and erythritol provide sweetness with fewer calories and minimal impact on blood sugar, making them popular in low-carb diets. Other types include syrups and polyols, which also contribute to health-focused formulations. Together, they enhance dietary supplements aimed at overall wellness.</p></p>
