<p><strong>The Automotive Active Window Display Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Combiner Technology</li><li>Windshield Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive active window display market primarily includes two technologies: combiner technology and windshield technology. Combiner technology utilizes a semi-transparent display that overlays information on the vehicle’s windshield for a heads-up display experience. In contrast, windshield technology integrates display elements directly into the windshield glass, providing a seamless digital interface that conveys critical driving information. Both technologies enhance driver awareness and safety by projecting essential data without obstructing the driver's view of the road.</p></p>
