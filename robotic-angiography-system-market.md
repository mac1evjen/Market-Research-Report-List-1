<p><strong>The Robotic Angiography System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The Robotic Angiography System market encompasses three main types: </p><p>1. **CT Angiography Systems** utilize computed tomography to create detailed images of blood vessels, aiding in diagnosing vascular conditions with high precision.</p><p>2. **X-ray Angiography Systems** employ fluoroscopy to visualize blood flow in real-time, commonly used for interventional procedures and diagnostics.</p><p>3. **MR Angiography Systems** leverage magnetic resonance imaging to produce images of blood vessels without ionizing radiation, offering a non-invasive option for assessing vascular health. </p><p>Together, these systems enhance diagnostic accuracy and treatment efficacy in vascular interventions.</p></p>
