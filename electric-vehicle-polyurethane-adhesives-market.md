<p><ul><li>Water-based Polyurethane Adhesive</li><li>Solvent-based Polyurethane Adhesive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Polyurethane Adhesives market includes two primary types: water-based and solvent-based polyurethane adhesives. Water-based polyurethane adhesives are eco-friendly, offering low volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and enhanced safety during application. They provide excellent bonding strength and flexibility for various components. In contrast, solvent-based polyurethane adhesives deliver superior performance in extreme conditions, often used for high-stress applications due to their strong adhesion and durability. Both types cater to the increasing demand for lightweight, efficient bonding solutions in electric vehicle manufacturing.</p></p>
