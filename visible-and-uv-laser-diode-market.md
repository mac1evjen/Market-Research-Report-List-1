<p><p>The Visible and UV Laser Diode market is segmented into single-mode and multi-mode types. Single-mode laser diodes emit light through a single path or mode, ensuring high precision and narrow bandwidth, making them suitable for applications like telecommunications and high-resolution imaging. Multi-mode laser diodes, on the other hand, allow multiple light paths, offering higher output power and broader beam widths, which benefit applications like pumping lasers and barcode scanners. Each type serves distinct applications based on performance requirements.</p></p>
