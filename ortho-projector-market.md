<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Ortho-Projector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Digital Light Processing (DLP)</li><li>Liquid Crystal Display (LCD)</li><li>Liquid Crystal on Silicon (LCoS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ortho-projector market encompasses three primary types: Digital Light Processing (DLP), Liquid Crystal Display (LCD), and Liquid Crystal on Silicon (LCoS). DLP utilizes micro-mirrors to create images, offering superior color accuracy and brightness, ideal for larger displays. LCD technology relies on liquid crystals, providing excellent image quality and affordability, commonly used in consumer projectors. LCoS combines the qualities of both DLP and LCD, featuring reflective liquid crystals for high resolution and sharp images, making it suitable for professional applications.</p></p>
