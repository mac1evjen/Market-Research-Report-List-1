<p><p>The Electronic Medical Records (EMR) software market primarily includes cloud-based and web-based solutions. Cloud-based EMR software stores patient data on remote servers, providing accessibility from various devices and enhanced data security. It typically offers lower upfront costs and easier scalability. In contrast, web-based EMR software operates through a browser, allowing real-time access without installation. While often requiring a stable internet connection, it facilitates seamless updates and minimal IT management, making it suitable for small to medium-sized healthcare providers.</p></p>
