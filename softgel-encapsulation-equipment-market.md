<p><p>The softgel encapsulation equipment market is divided into two main types: manual encapsulation equipment and automatic encapsulation equipment. Manual encapsulation equipment is designed for small-scale production and typically requires operator intervention for filling and sealing softgels. Conversely, automatic encapsulation equipment is intended for large-scale manufacturing, offering higher efficiency and consistent output with minimal human involvement. Both types cater to different production needs, with manual equipment suited for smaller batches and customization, while automatic systems optimize speed and volume for mass production.</p></p>
