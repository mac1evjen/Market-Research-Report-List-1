<p><p>The Automotive Condition Monitoring System market is divided into two main types: Cloud Systems and Local Systems. Cloud Systems utilize internet connectivity to collect and analyze vehicle data remotely, allowing real-time monitoring and advanced analytics for predictive maintenance. In contrast, Local Systems operate directly within the vehicle using onboard sensors and diagnostics, providing immediate feedback without external connectivity. Both systems aim to enhance vehicle performance, optimize maintenance schedules, and improve safety through constant monitoring of vehicle health.</p></p>
