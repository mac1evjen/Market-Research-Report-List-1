<p><p>The CNC machine tool blade market encompasses various types of cutting tools essential for precision manufacturing. Turning tool blades are designed for shaping materials by rotating them against a fixed cutting tool, ideal for cylindrical parts. Drilling tool blades are optimized for creating holes in a range of materials, typically featuring sharp, spiral designs for efficient chip removal. Milling tool blades are versatile tools used for shaping surfaces through rotary motion, allowing for complex cuts and profiles in various applications across industries.</p></p>
